Chosen Category

Our chosen category is film and animation with a topic revolving around horror. Our target audience are students from APEC in the grades 9 to 10, based on our survey.. we have a total of 8 respondents from grade 9, section 1. 6 respondents from grade 9 section 2. And 2 respondents from grade 10 section 1.
